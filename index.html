About Us

Chartered on June 9th, 1996, Lambda Phi Epsilon's Alpha Beta Chapter at New York University is an Asian interest social fraternity that has since grown to over 200 lifelong members.

The goals of our chapter includes, but is not limited to, developing active members to become leaders, advancing personal growth, academic achievement, and promoting Asian American awareness on campus. We also give back to the community through philanthropy organizations such as the Asian-American Bone Marrow Donor Program and Relay for Life.

Our alumni and even some of our current brothers are extremely successful, having worked at companies such as Google, J.P. Morgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, Amazon, Morgan Stanley, Deloitte, and Bank of America. By joining us, you will be joining an extensive network that reaches across the nation and beyond. To top it all off, we interact and mix heavily with numerous sororities from NYU and other schools along the East Coast.

By joining us, you will be gaining lifelong relationships, networking and career opportunities, and an overall more fulfilling college career. Check out the rush schedule to find out more!
